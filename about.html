About her:

  • Born in 5th November 1913, British India.
  • Her real name was "Vivian Mary Hartley".
  • One of the famous movie that starred was: "Gone With The Wind" in 1939.
  • She earned a reputation for being difficult to work with.
  • She had bipolar disorder, as well as recurrent bouts of chronic tuberculosis, which was first diagnosed in the mid-1940s and ultimately killed her at the age of 53.
